### 一、最常用介词：at / on / in（美式英语）

| 介词 | 适用场景 | 示例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **at** | 具体时间点、时刻 | at 6:00 / at noon / at midnight / at night |
| **on** | 具体某一天、日期、星期几 | on Monday / on May 5th / on my birthday |
| **on** | 周末 | on the weekend / on weekends |
| **on** | **工作日（特指某一天）** | **on a weekday** |
| **in** | 较长时段：月、年、季节、上午/下午/傍晚 | in May / in 2026 / in summer / in the morning |
| **in** | 一周内（泛指某周） | in a week / in the first week of May |
| **during** | 在一周期间（强调过程） | during the week |
| **during** | **在工作日期间** | **during the week / during weekdays** |

> ✅ **美式英语关键点**：
> - 表达“在周末”统一用 **on**。
> - 表达“在工作日（特指某一天）”用 **on a weekday**。
> - 表达“在工作日期间（泛指）”用 **during the week** 或 **during weekdays**。---


### 二、表示“在……期间”

| 短语 | 含义 | 示例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **during** | 在……期间（强调过程） | during the meeting / during the vacation |
| **throughout** | 贯穿整个期间（强调从头到尾） | throughout the day / throughout the year |
| **over** | 在……期间（常与一段时间连用） | over the weekend / over the past year |
| **during** | 在一周期间 | during the week |
| **during** | **在工作日期间** | **during the week / during weekdays** |---


### 三、表示“持续一段时间”

| 短语 | 含义 | 示例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **for** | 持续……时间（后接时间段长度） | for two hours / for a week / for years |
| **since** | 自从……（后接时间点） | since Monday / since 2020 |
| **from ... to ...** | 从……到…… | from Monday to Friday / from 9 am to 5 pm |
| **on weekdays** | **在每个工作日（泛指）** | **I work on weekdays.** |---


### 四、表示“在……之后 / 之前 / 之内”

| 短语 | 含义 | 示例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **before** | 在……之前 | before noon / before the meeting |
| **after** | 在……之后 | after 6 pm / after work |
| **within** | 在……之内（不超过） | within a week / within 24 hours |
| **by** | 不迟于……（截止时间） | by Friday / by next month |
| **in** | 在……之后（将来时） | in a week（一周后） |---


### 五、表示“每隔一段时间”

| 短语 | 含义 | 示例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **every** | 每…… | every day / every two weeks / every weekend |
| **every other** | 每隔一…… | every other day / every other weekend |
| **once/twice a week** | 每周一/两次 | once a week / twice a week |
| **on weekdays** | **在每个工作日** | **The gym opens at 6 am on weekdays.** |---


### 六、常见错误修正表（美式英语，含 weekday）

| ❌ 错误说法 | ✅ 正确说法（美式） | 说明 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| at the week | during the week / in the week | 周用 during 或 in |
| at the morning | in the morning | 上午/下午/傍晚用 in |
| on night | at night | 夜里用 at |
| on summer | in summer | 季节用 in |
| in Monday | on Monday | 星期几用 on |
| at the weekend / at weekends | on the weekend / on weekends | 美式英语 weekend 统一用 on |
| in weekend | on the weekend | weekend 不用 in |
| on that week | in that week | 某周内用 in |
| **at weekday** | **on a weekday / on weekdays** | weekday 用 on |
| **in weekdays** | **on weekdays / during weekdays** | weekday 不用 in |---


### 七、week / weekend / weekday 常见搭配速查（美式英语）

| 你想表达的意思 | 美式英语 |
| :--- | :--- |
| 在一周内（泛指某周） | in a week / in the first week |
| 在这一周期间 | during the week |
| 整个星期 | throughout the week |
| 一周后（将来） | in a week |
| 一周之内（不超过） | within a week |
| 每周一/两次 | once a week / twice a week |
| 在周末（特指某个周末） | on the weekend |
| 在周末（泛指/每逢周末） | on weekends |
| 整个周末 | throughout the weekend |
| 过了这个周末 | over the weekend |
| **在工作日（特指某一天）** | **on a weekday** |
| **在工作日（泛指/每逢工作日）** | **on weekdays** |
| **在工作日期间** | **during the week / during weekdays** |---
