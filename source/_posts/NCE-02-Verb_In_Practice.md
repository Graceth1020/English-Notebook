# Verbs collocated with **in**
> Format: Collocation + Chinese meaning + Example sentence (complex structure & varied tenses)

1. **believe in** 信仰；信任

   Most people around the world believe in kindness and honesty, which have been valued for thousands of years.

2. **delight in** 喜爱；乐于

   The elderly couple delight in walking through the quiet garden and chatting with neighbors every sunny morning.

3. **be employed in** 从事；受雇于

   He has been employed in medical research for over ten years and has made many important discoveries.

4. **encourage sb in** 在……方面鼓励某人

   My teachers always encourage me in my artistic creation whenever I feel upset about my works.

5. **be engaged in** 忙于；从事；正在做

   When I visited her yesterday, she was fully engaged in preparing for the upcoming national competition.

6. **be experienced in** 在……方面有经验

   The senior engineer is highly experienced in dealing with all kinds of complicated mechanical failures.

7. **fail in** 在……失败；未能做到

   Though he failed in the final exam last term, he never gave up and kept working harder day by day.

8. **help in/with** 帮助；协助
   Many volunteers came to help in the community service after the heavy rain destroyed several local houses.

9. **include in** 把……包含在内

   All your travel expenses will be included in the total bill that we will send you next Monday.

10. **indulge in** 沉迷；沉醉于

    You should not indulge in online games for too long, or it will affect your study and physical health.

11. **instruct sb in** 在……方面教导某人

    A professional coach will instruct the new players in basic skills before the official training starts.

12. **be interested in** 对……感兴趣

    More and more young people are becoming interested in traditional culture and folk art in recent years.

13. **invest in** 投资；投入

    Many wise businessmen choose to invest in environmental industries for long-term stable development.

14. **be involved in** 卷入；参与

    No one wants to be involved in unnecessary arguments that will waste plenty of time and energy.

15. **persist in** 坚持

    As long as you persist in practicing every day, you will finally master this foreign language well.

16. **share in** 分享；分担

    True friends will always share in both your joy and sorrow throughout your whole life.---
