### 1、Examples
- curious about(对……好奇的): Children, who are always full of imagination, are curious about everything that happens around them.
- doubtful about/of(对……有疑问): Many people are doubtful about the plan that the company put forward last week due to its high risk.
- enthusiastic about(对……热心): She is enthusiastic about the volunteer work that can help people in need in the community.
- reluctant about/to(勉强): He is reluctant about the decision that requires him to move to another city far from his family.
- right about/in(在……是正确的): You are right about the result of the experiment that we have been working on for several months.
- uneasy about(对……感到不安): She feels uneasy about the news that her son will travel abroad alone for the first time.

### 2、Exercises
#### 2.1
1. Young people are curious __ the unknown world that lies beyond their daily experience.
2. Some experts are doubtful __ the effectiveness of the new policy aimed at reducing pollution.
3. The students are enthusiastic __ the cultural exchange activity organized by their school.
4. He is reluctant __ the long journey that will take him away from his hometown for a year.
5. The manager is right __ the way to solve the problem that has troubled the team for a long time.
6. Parents often feel uneasy __ the safety of their children when they go out alone at night.

#### 2.2
1. 年轻人对超出他们日常经验之外的未知世界充满好奇。
2. 一些专家对这项旨在减少污染的新政策的有效性持怀疑态度。
3. 学生们对学校组织的这次文化交流活动非常热心。
4. 他对这次将使他离开家乡一年的长途旅行感到勉强。
5. 经理在解决这个困扰团队许久的问题上是正确的。
6. 当孩子们晚上独自外出时，父母们常常会为他们的安全感到不安。
