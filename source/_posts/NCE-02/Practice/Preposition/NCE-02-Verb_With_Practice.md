# Verbs + with
## 一、Examples
1. **agree with 同意（观点/人）**
   Most experts agree with the new theory that researchers have put forward in recent years.
2. **begin with 以……开始**
   The opening ceremony will begin with a wonderful folk dance prepared by local performers.
3. **communicate with 和……沟通**
   Scientists have communicated with overseas partners to exchange latest research data for months.
4. **compare with/to 和……对比**
   If you compare this new model with the old one, you will find obvious improvements in performance.
5. **compete with/against 和……竞争**
   Small brands have to compete with large multinational companies in the fierce market.
6. **comply with 遵从，遵守**
   All factories must comply with environmental laws that the government issued last year.
7. **confuse … with 把……错当成**
   People often confuse this similar-looking plant with another poisonous wild herb.
8. **contrast with/to 和……形成鲜明对比**
   Her calm attitude contrasts sharply with the nervous mood of other participants in the interview.
9. **cope with 处理、应付**
   He has learned how to cope with various unexpected troubles after years of working experience.
10. **correspond with 与……相符；通信**
    The actual result does not correspond with the prediction they made at the beginning.
11. **be disgusted with 厌恶**
    She was disgusted with his lazy habits after living together for half a year.
12. **finish with 做完；结束**
    I will finish with all paperwork before my supervisor comes back from a business trip.
13. **help with/in 在……方面帮忙**
    Many volunteers help with daily care for the elderly in community nursing centers.
14. **interfere with/in 打扰、干涉**
    Never interfere with others’ private choices unless they ask you for advice actively.
15. **mix with 混合**
    You cannot mix this chemical liquid with water, or dangerous gas will come out.
16. **be occupied with 忙于，从事**
    The engineer has been occupied with a key project since the start of this quarter.
17. **part with 舍弃、忍痛放弃**
    He refused to part with the old watch which was left by his late grandfather.
18. **be pleased with 对……满意**
    The manager was pleased with the sales volume his team achieved last month.
19. **quarrel with/about 和……争吵**
    They never quarrel with each other about trivial family matters in daily life.
20. **reason with 规劝、讲道理**
    Teachers tried to reason with the naughty student to correct his bad behaviors patiently.
21. **be satisfied with/by 对……满足**
    Customers are satisfied with the thoughtful after-sales service provided by this brand.
22. **be threatened with 受到……威胁**
    Many wild animals were threatened with extinction due to continuous environmental damage.

## 二、Exercises
### 2.1 Preposition blank filling
1. Few people agree ____ his unreasonable opinion on the company’s future development.
2. Every English class usually begins ____ a short English song in this school.
3. It is convenient to communicate ____ foreign friends by online video calls nowadays.
4. Don’t compare your own progress ____ others’ results blindly.
5. Domestic products need to compete ____ imported goods in global markets.
6. Every employee has to comply ____ the safety rules set by the factory.
7. Don’t confuse this word ____ another one which has close spelling.
8. The cold winter scenery contrasts greatly ____ the warm view in summer.
9. It is tough to cope ____ sudden emergencies when you work alone on night shifts.
10. What you said doesn’t correspond ____ the real fact of the whole incident.
11. She is disgusted ____ the dirty environment around her living neighborhood.
12. I hope to finish ____ all household chores before my parents arrive home.
13. Several classmates often help me ____ my difficult math exercises.
14. It’s rude to interfere ____ other people’s personal plans without permission.
15. Stir well when you mix the powder ____ warm boiled water.
16. My father is fully occupied ____ preparing for an important business meeting recently.
17. It is hard for him to part ____ his beloved pet dog.
18. The boss was pleased ____ the excellent work finished ahead of schedule.
19. They quarrelled ____ each other about how to arrange the coming holiday.
20. We spent hours reasoning ____ the child to give up risky outdoor games.
21. Most passengers are satisfied ____ the tidy and comfortable service on this train.
22. The small village was threatened ____ flood after days of heavy rainfall.

### 2.2 Answers（统一介词with）
1.with 2.with 3.with 4.with 5.with/against 6.with 7.with 8.with/to 9.with
10.with 11.with 12.with 13.with/in 14.with/in 15.with 16.with 17.with
18.with 19.with 20.with 21.with/by 22.with
