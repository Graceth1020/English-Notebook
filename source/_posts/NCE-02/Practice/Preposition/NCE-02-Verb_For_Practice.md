# Verbs collocated with for
## 一、Examples
1. **account for 说明原因、解释**
   Nobody could account for the strange mistakes that had appeared in the official report.
2. **ask for/of 请求**
   Many local farmers asked for practical support from the government after the heavy flood.
3. **act for/on 代表；按照…行动**
   The experienced lawyer will act for the young man in the upcoming court trial.
4. **apologize for 因……道歉**
   He apologized sincerely for the rude words he had said to his teammates the day before.
5. **blame for 因…责备**
   Don’t blame him for the failure, for most troubles were caused by unexpected outside factors.
6. **beg for 乞求**
   The homeless children begged for food and warm clothes on the cold winter street years ago.
7. **call for 需要、要求**
   This tough project calls for plenty of patience and professional skills from every member.
8. **charge for 收费**
   The restaurant never charges customers for plain drinking water or small side dishes.
9. **exchange for 交换**
   She exchanged her old useless books for a set of newly published storybooks last weekend.
10. **hope for 期盼**
    All the injured people hope for a quick recovery and a peaceful life in the future.
11. **look for 寻找**
    We have been looking for useful information to finish our research paper these days.
12. **mistake for 错当成**
    People once mistook the strange rock for an ancient stone statue hundreds of years ago.
13. **mourn for 哀悼**
    The whole nation mourned for those heroes who lost their lives protecting people’s safety.
14. **pay for 付钱；为…付出代价**
    You will pay for your careless decisions once you realize all the bad results later.
15. **prepare for 为…做准备**
    All athletes are busy preparing for the important sports competition to be held next month.
16. **provide for 供养、供给**
    He worked day and night to provide for his big family after his father passed away.
17. **search for 搜寻**
    Rescuers kept searching for survivors trapped under the broken buildings after the disaster.
18. **thank for 因…感谢**
    I must thank you for the valuable advice you offered me when I was in trouble.
19. **vote for/on 投票支持**
    Most representatives voted for the new environmental protection rule at yesterday’s meeting.
20. **wait for/on 等候；伺候**
    We had to wait for nearly two hours because the train was delayed by heavy rain.

## 二、Exercises

8. Shops seldom charge customers ____ paper bags in many modern cities now.

10. Everyone hopes ____ fine weather for the coming school hiking activity.
11. They spent the whole afternoon looking ____ the lost key around the neighborhood.
12. I mistook the tall stranger ____ my former high school teacher just now.
