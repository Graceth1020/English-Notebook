# Verbs collocated with **from**
> Format: Collocation + Chinese meaning + Example sentence (complex structure & varied tenses)

1. **borrow sth from sb** 从……借

   Since I left my notebook at home this morning, I have borrowed several reference materials from my classmates to finish today’s class tasks.

2. **defend sb/sth from/against** 保护……使免于

   Throughout history, brave soldiers have always stood firmly to defend their motherland against foreign invasion and external threats.

3. **demand sth from/of sb** 向……要求

   Many experts point out that we should never demand perfection of young people while they are still learning and growing.

4. **differ from** 有别于；与……不同

   The living habits in southern China differ greatly from those in northern areas because of the huge differences in climate and geography.

5. **dismiss sb from** 解雇；使离职

   He was dismissed from his position last month because he had neglected his duties and violated the company’s regulations repeatedly.

6. **draw sth from** 从……中得出

   After analyzing a large amount of data, researchers have drawn valuable conclusions from this long-term social survey.

7. **emerge from** 从……出现；浮现

   When the heavy fog gradually lifted at dawn, a small beautiful village emerged from the rolling mountains in the distance.

8. **escape from** 从……逃出；逃离

   By the time the firefighters arrived, only a few residents had managed to escape from the suddenly caught fire apartment building.

9. **excuse sb from/for** 允许不做……；原谅

   The teacher will excuse me from tomorrow’s sports meeting because I hurt my ankle when I ran on the playground yesterday.

10. **hinder sb from** 阻止；阻碍

    If you always lack self-confidence, it will hinder you from seizing precious opportunities that may change your whole life.

11. **prevent sb from** 妨碍；阻止

    No matter what difficulties we will meet in the future, nothing can prevent us from pursuing our original dreams bravely.

12. **prohibit sb from** 不准许；禁止

    The newly issued school rules prohibit all students from carrying electronic devices into classrooms during formal lessons.

13. **protect sb/sth from/against** 保护……免受伤害

    People living near the coast usually build solid walls to protect their houses against the powerful winds caused by typhoons every summer.

14. **receive sth from sb** 接到；收到

    I have received dozens of warm postcards from my former classmates since we graduated from high school three years ago.

15. **separate A from B** 把……分开；分离

    It is not easy for ordinary readers to separate real facts from false information when browsing various news online.

16. **suffer from** 遭受；受……折磨

    A great number of elderly people in this region have suffered from serious joint pain ever since the cold rainy season began last week.---
