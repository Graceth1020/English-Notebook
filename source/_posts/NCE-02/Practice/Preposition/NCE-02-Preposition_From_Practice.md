# 1、Examples
- away from(距……远的): The new school is far away from my home, so I have to get up early every morning.
- different from(与……不同的): The lifestyle in the city is quite different from that in the countryside.
- far from(远离): What he did is far from what he promised to us before.
- safe from(无危险的): The little village is safe from storms because it is surrounded by high mountains.

# 2、Exercises
## 2.1
1. The beach is miles away __ our hotel, so we need to take a bus.
2. His idea is totally different __ mine on this project.
3. The result is far __ what we expected at the beginning.
4. The kids are safe __ any harm in this protected area.

## 2.2
1. 海滩离我们的酒店有好几英里远，所以我们需要坐公交车。
2. 在这个项目上，他的想法和我的完全不同。
3. 这个结果与我们最初期待的相去甚远。
4. 孩子们在这个保护区里不会受到任何伤害。
