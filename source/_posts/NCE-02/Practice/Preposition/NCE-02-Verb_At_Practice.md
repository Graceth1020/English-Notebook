# Verbs collocated with at
## 1. Examples 
- **amused at/by** (对……感到有趣)
  We were greatly amused at the funny stories that the old coach told during the break.
- **arrive at/in** (到达)
  After a long journey, our group finally arrived at the remote village before sunset.
- **astonished at/by** (感到惊愕)
  Everyone was astonished by the amazing progress he had made in such a short period of time.
- **exclaim at** (惊叫)
  The tourists exclaimed at the spectacular scenery spread out in front of them on the mountain top.
- **glance at** (看一眼)
  She glanced at her watch quietly while listening to the long speech from the manager.
- **guess at** (猜测)
  No one can exactly guess at the real reason why he suddenly gave up this golden chance.
- **knock at** (敲)
  Someone knocked at the door repeatedly when we were discussing important matters last night.
- **look at** (看)
  You should look at problems from different angles instead of sticking to one single view.
- **point at/to** (指向)
  The tour guide pointed to an ancient tower and introduced its long history to all visitors.
- **shocked at/by** (感到震惊)
  Local people were shocked at the serious damage caused by the sudden natural disaster.
- **stare at** (盯着……看)
  It is impolite to stare at strangers for a long time when you walk on the street.
- **surprised at/by** (感到惊讶)
  Her parents were deeply surprised by her decision to take part in the volunteer program abroad.
- **wonder at/about** (感到惊异)
  Scientists still wonder at the mysterious natural phenomena that exist deep in the ocean.
- **work at/on** (钻研；致力于)
  He has been working at professional knowledge day and night to finish the research project.

## 2. Exercises
### 2.1 Fill in the blanks
1. All the audience were amused ____ the actor’s vivid performance on the stage.
2. We will arrive ____ the airport two hours ahead of the departure time.
3. People were astonished ____ the huge change of this small town over ten years.
4. The children exclaimed ____ the beautiful fireworks lighting up the night sky.
5. He glanced ____ the document quickly and then handed it back to his colleague.
6. It’s hard for us to guess ____ what will happen in the future.
7. A stranger knocked ____ my window when I was reading indoors this afternoon.
8. Please look ____ the details carefully before you sign your name on the contract.
9. The little boy pointed ____ the lovely cat running across the garden.
10. The whole class was shocked ____ the unexpected result of the competition.
11. Don’t stare ____ others. It is considered bad manners in public.
12. I was surprised ____ how quickly she adapted to the new living environment.
13. Many people wonder ____ the secrets behind this ancient building.
14. Most experts keep working ____ new technologies to improve people’s lives.
