### 1、Examples
- dependent on(依赖于……): The success of this project is dependent on the support that we receive from our partners.
- intent on(专心于……): He is intent on finishing the research paper that is due at the end of this month.
- keen on(热心于……): Many young people are keen on outdoor activities that can help them relax and keep fit.

### 2、Exercises
#### 2.1
1. The survival of small animals is dependent __ the natural environment around them.
2. She is intent __ learning French well so that she can study abroad next year.
3. My classmates are keen __ the basketball match to be held this weekend.
