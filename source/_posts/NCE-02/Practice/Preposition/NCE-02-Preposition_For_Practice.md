### 1、Examples
- eager for(渴望): Although she has faced many setbacks, she is still eager for a life that is full of challenges and opportunities.
- enough for(足够): The money that we have saved over the years is enough for a trip around Europe which we have been dreaming of for a long time.
- famous for(以……而闻名): Paris, which is one of the most romantic cities in the world, is famous for its exquisite art and unique architecture.
- fit for(合适): The new office building, which was designed by a renowned architect, is fit for a multinational company that has high standards for workspace.
- grateful for(因……而感激): After going through the difficult period, she is grateful for the support that her family and friends gave her when she was in trouble.
- grateful to(因……而感激): He is grateful to the teacher who spent extra time helping him with his studies when he fell behind the class.
- qualified for(能胜任): She is qualified for the position of project manager because she has the professional skills and experience that the job requires.
- qualified in(能胜任): The engineer is qualified in mechanical design, which makes him the best candidate for the technical director role in the factory.
- ready for(准备好): By the time the deadline comes, we will be ready for the presentation that will determine whether we can win the big client.
- ready to(准备好): As soon as the rain stops, the rescue team is ready to set off for the mountain area where the villagers are trapped.
- responsible for(对……负责): The department manager is responsible for the project that aims to improve the company's market share in the Asian market.
- sorry for(对……感到遗憾): I am sorry for the mistake that I made in the report, which caused unnecessary trouble for the whole team.
- sufficient for(充足的): The supplies that we have stored in the warehouse are sufficient for the production needs of the next three months even if the supply chain is disrupted.
- thankful for(感谢): The old man is thankful for the care that the community workers have provided to him since his wife passed away.
- thankful to(感谢): We are thankful to the volunteers who dedicated their time and energy to helping us organize the charity event successfully.
- valid for(有效期为……): This visa, which is issued by the embassy, is valid for 90 days from the date of issue, during which you can travel freely within the country.

### 2、Exercises
#### 2.1
1. Despite all the difficulties in her career, she is eager __ a chance to prove her ability in the international market.
2. The resources that the government has allocated are enough __ the reconstruction of the disaster-stricken areas that were severely damaged by the earthquake.
3. Venice, which is known as the "City of Water", is famous __ its ancient canals and unique cultural heritage.
4. The new safety standards ensure that the product is fit __ the harsh working environments that are common in the mining industry.
5. After receiving the scholarship, the student is grateful __ the foundation that has been supporting outstanding young talents from poor families.
6. The young doctor is qualified __ the role of chief physician in the hospital because of his rich clinical experience and professional knowledge.
7. After months of preparation, the team is ready __ the launch of the new product that is expected to change the market pattern.
8. The CEO of the company is responsible __ the strategic decisions that will affect the long-term development of the enterprise in the global market.
9. He is sorry __ the misunderstanding that he caused between his friends, which almost ruined their decades-long friendship.
10. The funds raised through the donation campaign are sufficient __ the medical treatment of all the sick children in the remote mountainous areas.
11. All the participants are thankful __ the professional guidance that the experts provided during the training program.
12. This official document, which is signed by the minister, is valid __ six months, within which you can complete all the required procedures.

#### 2.2
1. 尽管在她的职业生涯中面临种种困难，她依然渴望有一个机会能在国际市场上证明自己的能力。
2. 政府划拨的资源足以用于重建那些在地震中严重受损的受灾地区。
3. 威尼斯，这座被誉为“水城”的城市，以其古老的运河和独特的文化遗产而闻名。
4. 新的安全标准确保该产品适用于采矿业中常见的恶劣工作环境。
5. 获得奖学金后，这名学生对一直资助贫困家庭优秀青年人才的基金会心怀感激。
6. 这位年轻医生凭借丰富的临床经验和专业知识，能够胜任医院主任医师的职位。
7. 经过数月准备，团队已为推出这款有望改变市场格局的新产品做好了准备。
8. 公司首席执行官负责制定那些会影响企业在全球市场中长期发展的战略决策。
9. 他为自己在朋友间造成的误会感到遗憾，这场误会几乎毁掉了他们数十年的友谊。
10. 通过募捐活动筹集的资金足以用于偏远山区所有患病儿童的医疗救治。
11. 所有参与者都感谢专家们在培训项目期间提供的专业指导。
12. 这份由部长签署的官方文件有效期为六个月，在此期间你可以办理所有必要手续。
