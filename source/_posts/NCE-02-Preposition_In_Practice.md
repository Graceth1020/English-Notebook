### 1、Examples
- deficient in(缺少): Many teenagers are deficient in sleep, which affects their physical and mental development.
- fortunate in(在……很幸运): She is fortunate in having a family that always supports her dreams.
- honest in(对……很诚实): A good leader should be honest in dealing with all members of the team.
- weak in(在……薄弱的): He is weak in English grammar, so he needs to practice more every day.

### 2、Exercises
#### 2.1
1. The soil in this area is deficient __ essential nutrients for plant growth.
2. You are fortunate __ having such kind and helpful friends around you.
3. He is honest __ all his business transactions with partners.
4. Most students are weak __ listening and speaking skills in a foreign language.

#### 2.2
1. 这个地区的土壤缺少植物生长所必需的营养物质。
2. 你很幸运，身边有这么善良且乐于助人的朋友。
3. 他在与合作伙伴的所有商业往来中都很诚实。
4. 大多数学生在外语的听说能力方面比较薄弱。
