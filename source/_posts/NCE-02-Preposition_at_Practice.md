### 1、Examples
- bad at(不善于……): He is bad at expressing his feelings even though he cares about others deeply.
- clever at(擅长): She is clever at solving math problems that most students find difficult to understand.
- efficient at(能胜任的): The secretary is efficient at dealing with all kinds of paperwork that the manager assigns every day.
- expert at/in(能熟练做……): He is expert at repairing electronic devices that have complicated structures and functions.
- good at(善于): My sister is good at playing the piano which she has practiced for more than ten years.
- indignant at(对……感到愤慨): People are indignant at the unfair news that has spread widely on the Internet.
- quick at(很快): The boy is quick at learning new languages that require good memory and pronunciation.
- sad at/about(因……而悲伤): She is sad at the bad news that her best friend cannot come to her birthday party.
- slow at(对……迟钝): Some students are slow at understanding grammar rules even if they listen carefully in class.
- skillful at/in(熟练): The artist is skillful at drawing portraits that look like real people.

### 2、Exercises
#### 2.1
1. Many students are bad __ taking exams although they study hard every day.
2. My brother is clever __ making tools that can help us work more easily.
3. She is efficient __ organizing activities that attract a lot of participants.
4. He is expert __ fixing cars that have serious mechanical problems.
5. Most children are good __ singing songs that they hear on TV or radio.
6. We are indignant __ the rude behavior that hurts others’ feelings.
7. He is quick __ understanding instructions that others explain to him.
8. She is sad __ the result that she failed the important test.
9. Some people are slow __ learning new skills that need repeated practice.
10. The worker is skillful __ operating machines that are used in the factory.

#### 2.2
1. 许多学生不善于考试，尽管他们每天都努力学习。
2. 我哥哥擅长制作能帮我们更轻松工作的工具。
3. 她能胜任组织吸引很多参与者的活动。
4. 他能熟练修理有严重机械故障的汽车。
5. 大多数孩子善于唱他们在电视或收音机里听到的歌曲。
6. 我们对伤害他人感情的粗鲁行为感到愤慨。
7. 他能很快理解别人给他解释的说明。
8. 她因考试不及格的结果感到悲伤。
9. 有些人对学习需要反复练习的新技能比较迟钝。
10. 这位工人熟练操作工厂里使用的机器。
