# Verbs collocated with on
> Format: Collocation + Chinese meaning + Example sentence (complex structure & varied tenses)

1. **act on** 遵照；奉行

   Once you have accepted the rules, you must act on them strictly no matter what difficulties you may face.

2. **be based on** 以……为基础

   This popular movie is based on a true story that happened in a small town decades ago.

3. **call on** 拜访；探望

   We plan to call on our former teacher who has retired and lives in the countryside this weekend.

4. **comment on** 评论；发表意见

   Many netizens began to comment on the new policy after it was officially released last week.

5. **concentrate on** 集中精力于

   If you want to get good grades, you need to concentrate on your studies instead of being distracted easily.

6. **congratulate sb on** 祝贺

   All classmates gathered together to congratulate her on winning the national writing competition.

7. **consult on/about** 商量；咨询

   Managers often consult experienced staff on practical problems before putting forward new plans.

8. **count on** 依靠；指望

   You can always count on your true friends when you are trapped in trouble and feel helpless.

9. **decide on** 选定；决定

   After comparing several travel routes, we finally decided on a trip to the coastal city.

10. **depend on** 依靠；取决于

    The success of this project largely depends on whether every member can cooperate closely with each other.

11. **economize on** 节约；节省

    We should learn to economize on water and electricity to protect the limited natural resources.

12. **embark on** 着手；开始从事

    After graduating from university, she embarked on a brand-new career in the field of public welfare.

13. **experiment on** 做实验；尝试

    Scientists have spent years experimenting on new materials to develop safer and more durable products.

14. **insist on** 坚持

    Though others held different opinions, he still insisted on carrying out his original idea to the end.

15. **lean on/against** 倚靠；靠在

    Tired from the long walk, she leaned against the old wall and enjoyed the gentle wind quietly.

16. **live on** 靠……为生；以……为食

    Many fishermen living along the river live on fishing and selling aquatic products all year round.

17. **operate on** 给……动手术；对……起作用

    The professional doctors will operate on the patient early tomorrow morning to remove the tumor.

18. **perform on/in** 表演；扮演

    A group of young artists will perform on the grand stage during the upcoming art festival.

19. **pride (oneself) on** 为……感到自豪

    He prides himself on always keeping his promises and treating everyone sincerely.

20. **rely on** 依靠；信赖

    Modern transportation systems rely on advanced technology to run safely and efficiently every day.

21. **vote on a motion / vote for someone** 对提案表决 / 为某人投票

    All members of the committee will vote on this important motion at tomorrow's meeting.

22. **write on/about** 撰写；记述

    Many journalists have written about the changes of this ancient city over the past twenty years.---
