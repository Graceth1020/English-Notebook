# Verbs collocated with of
> Format: Collocation + Chinese meaning + Example sentence

1. **accuse sb of sth** 控告；指控某人做某事
   The police accused the young man of stealing goods from the supermarket last week.

2. **approve of sb./sth** 赞成；认可
   Most teachers and parents do not approve of students spending too much time on online games.

3. **assure sb of sth** 让……放心；向某人保证某事
   The manager assured us of full support when we started carrying out the new project.

4. **beware of sb./sth** 谨防；当心
   Tourists are reminded to beware of pickpockets while traveling in crowded scenic spots.

5. **boast of/about sth** 夸耀；吹嘘
   He has never boasted of his achievements even though he has made great progress in his career.

6. **complain of/about sth** 埋怨；抱怨
   Many residents have complained about the noisy construction work which has lasted for weeks.

7. **consist of sth** 由……组成
   This traditional snack consists of simple ingredients that can be easily found in daily life.

8. **convince sb of/about sth** 使某人信服某事
   She managed to convince everyone of the truth after showing enough solid evidence.

9. **cure sb of sth** 治愈（某人的疾病/陋习）
   The professional doctor finally cured him of the strange illness that had troubled him for years.

10. **despair of sb/sth** 对……丧失希望
    No matter how many difficulties we meet, we should never despair of our bright future.

11. **dream of/about sth** 幻想；梦想
    Since she was a little girl, she has dreamed of becoming a professional painter traveling around the world.

12. **expect sth of/from sb** 对某人抱有期望
    What parents expect most from their children is not wealth but a healthy and positive mindset.

13. **hear of sth** 听说；得知消息 / **hear from sb** 收到某人来信/消息
    I had never heard of this remote mountain village until my teacher mentioned it in class yesterday.

14. **be/get rid of sb/sth** 摆脱；除去
    It will take you a long time to get rid of the bad living habits you have formed for many years.

15. **smell of sth** 有……气味；闻到
    The old wooden house smells of dry grass and sunshine after being cleaned thoroughly.

16. **suspect sb of sth** 猜疑；怀疑某人做某事
    People suspected him of hiding important information during the whole investigation process.

17. **think of/about sb/sth** 思考；想到
    When we are making important decisions, we need to think of the influence on people around us.

18. **be tired of sb/sth** 对……感到厌烦
    Most people will be tired of doing the same repetitive work day after day without any change.

19. **warn sb of/against sth** 警告某人（有危险）
    The weather forecast warned all citizens of the coming heavy storm this evening.---
