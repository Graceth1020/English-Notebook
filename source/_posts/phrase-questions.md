### 1、challenge to

**释义**：  
作为动词短语的一部分，通常有两种用法：

1. **challenge someone to + noun**：向某人发出……挑战（名词为挑战内容，如比赛、决斗等）
2. **challenge someone to do something**：激励/要求某人做某事，考验其能力

**注意**：单独的 “challenge to” 不完整，需要接宾语或动词。

**造句**：
- He **challenged me to** a chess game.  
  （他向我发起国际象棋挑战。）
- The teacher **challenged us to** finish the project in two days.  
  （老师激励我们两天内完成项目。）
- She felt **challenged to** prove her skills.  
  （她感到被考验，需要证明自己的能力。）

### 2、mention to

**释义**：  
动词短语，表示“向某人提及/谈到某事”。  
用法：**mention + 某事 + to + 某人** 或 **mention to + 某人 + that从句**。

注意：mention 后面 **不加介词 about**（❌ mention about something → ✅ mention something）。---


### 结构分析：

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| mention + 某事 + to + 某人 | *Did you mention the meeting time **to her**?* |
| mention to + 某人 + that从句 | *He **mentioned to me that** he would be late.* |
| mention + that从句（省略to sb） | *She **mentioned that** she was tired.* |
| be mentioned to sb | *This was never **mentioned to us** before.* |---


### 注意区分：

| 短语 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| mention to someone | 向某人提及某事 |
| mention something | 提起某事（不指定对象） |
| not to mention（固定短语） | 更不用说 / 此外还有 |---


### 造句：

1. *I **mentioned to my boss** that I needed a day off.*  
   （我向老板提过我需要请一天假。）

2. *Did she **mention the party to you**?*  
   （她向你提起过派对的事吗？）

3. *He never **mentioned to his parents** that he had lost his job.*  
   （他从未向父母提起过他失业了。）

4. *The instructions were **mentioned to us** only once.*  
   （说明只向我们提过一次。）

5. *His name was **mentioned to** me by a colleague.*  
   （是一个同事向我提起他的名字的。）---


### ⚠️ 常见错误：

❌ *He mentioned about the meeting to me.*  
✅ *He mentioned the meeting to me.*

❌ *I mentioned to her about the problem.*  
✅ *I mentioned the problem to her.*---
