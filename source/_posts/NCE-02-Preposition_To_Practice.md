### 1、Examples
- close to(接近于): The temperature here **has been** close to 40℃ for a week, so many people **stay** at home.
- contrary to(与……相反): Contrary to what he **said**, he **didn’t show** up at the meeting yesterday.
- cruel to(对……残忍): It **is** shameful that anyone **is** cruel to animals that can’t protect themselves.
- dear to(对……很重要): Her family’s support **has always been** dear to her since she **left** home.
- equal to(与……相等): The new task **will be** equal to the one we **finished** last month.
- faithful to(忠于……): He **remained** faithful to his promise even when he **faced** great difficulties.
- fatal to(对……是致命的): This kind of mistake **was** fatal to the project that we **had planned** for months.
- harmful to(对……有害的): Smoking **is** harmful to health, as everyone **knows**.
- identical to(与……相同的): The copy **looks** identical to the original painting that the artist **created**.
- indifferent to(对……不关心): She **seems** indifferent to the fame that many people **are eager for**.
- inferior to(劣于……): This product **is** not inferior to any other brand that **sells** well on the market.
- liable to(对……有义务的): You **will be** liable to the consequences if you **break** the rules.
- new to(对……没有经验): He **was** new to the job when he **first came** to the company.
- obedient to(对……服从): The students **are** obedient to their teachers who **teach** them carefully.
- obvious to(对……清楚的): His effort **has been** obvious to everyone since the term **started**.
- polite to(对……有礼貌): We **should** be polite to everyone we **meet** in daily life.
- previous to(先于): Previous to the meeting, we **had collected** enough data that **was needed**.
- rude to(对……粗暴无礼): He **apologized** because he **had been** rude to his colleague that morning.
- sensitive to(对……敏感): She **is** very sensitive to the weather that **changes** quickly.
- similar to(与……相似): Your idea **is** similar to mine that we **discussed** last week.
- useful to(对……有用): This book **will be** useful to anyone who **wants** to improve writing skills.

### 2、Exercises
#### 2.1
1. The river is close ____ the village where we once lived.
2. Contrary to our prediction, the plan ____ rejected last Friday.
3. We should not be cruel ____ those who are weak and helpless.
4. Good memories ____ always dear ____ my heart.
5. The two boxes ____ equal ____ each other in size and weight.
6. He ____ faithful to his friends even when they ____ in trouble.
7. This chemical ____ fatal to small insects if used in large amounts.
8. Staying up too late ____ harmful to your physical health.
9. Your handwriting is identical ____ mine in many ways.
10. She ____ indifferent to the prizes that she ____ won last term.
11. My composition is not inferior ____ yours in content and structure.
12. Drivers ____ liable ____ traffic laws when they drive on the road.
13. I ____ new to this work when I ____ employed last year.
14. Children should be obedient ____ their parents and teachers.
15. The truth ____ obvious to everyone who ____ present at the scene.
16. You should be polite ____ everyone no matter who you are.
17. Previous to the exam, we ____ reviewed all the lessons carefully.
18. He ____ sorry that he ____ rude to the waiter yesterday.
19. Some people ____ sensitive to strong light and loud noise.
20. His teaching style is similar ____ that of my former teacher.
21. This advice ____ very useful to us when we ____ in trouble.
