**Countable vs. Uncountable: Hundred, Thousand, Million, Billion**

This note explains when to add **-s** to these number words and when to keep them singular.

---

## The Golden Rule (Applies to All)

> **If you put a specific number before it (two, five, ten) → NO "s"**  
> **If you mean "many" without a specific number → ADD "s" + "of"**

---

## 1. Hundred

| Usage | Countable? | Example | Rule |
|-------|------------|---------|------|
| With a specific number (100, 200, 500) | ❌ **Uncountable** (no -s) | *three **hundred** people* | Hundred stays singular |
| Without a number, meaning "many groups of 100" | ✅ **Countable** (with -s) | ***hundreds of** people* | Add -s and use "of" |
| As a noun meaning "100-unit bills/groups" | ✅ **Countable** | *He paid me in **hundreds*** | Can be plural |

**Examples:**
- *I need **two hundred** dollars.* (specific → no -s)
- ***Hundreds of** fans waited outside.* (vague → add -s)
- *She gave me two **hundreds**.* (two 100-dollar bills → countable plural)

---

## 2. Thousand

| Usage | Countable? | Example | Rule |
|-------|------------|---------|------|
| With a specific number (1,000, 5,000, 10,000) | ❌ **Uncountable** (no -s) | *five **thousand** miles* | Thousand stays singular |
| Without a number, meaning "many groups of 1,000" | ✅ **Countable** (with -s) | ***thousands of** people* | Add -s and use "of" |
| As a noun meaning "1,000-unit groups" | ✅ **Countable** | *The cost runs into the **thousands*** | Can be plural |

**Examples:**
- *She earns **three thousand** dollars a month.* (specific → no -s)
- ***Thousands of** tourists visit every year.* (vague → add -s)
- *The repair will cost **thousands**.* (vague plural noun)

---

## 3. Million

| Usage | Countable? | Example | Rule |
|-------|------------|---------|------|
| With a specific number (1M, 5M, 10M) | ❌ **Uncountable** (no -s) | *ten **million** people* | Million stays singular |
| Without a number, meaning "many groups of 1,000,000" | ✅ **Countable** (with -s) | ***millions of** stars* | Add -s and use "of" |
| As a noun meaning "1,000,000-unit groups" | ✅ **Countable** | *He's worth **millions*** | Can be plural |

**Examples:**
- *The company made **five million** dollars.* (specific → no -s)
- ***Millions of** viewers watched the final.* (vague → add -s)
- *She inherited **millions** from her uncle.* (vague plural noun)

---

## 4. Billion

| Usage | Countable? | Example | Rule |
|-------|------------|---------|------|
| With a specific number (1B, 3B, 7B) | ❌ **Uncountable** (no -s) | *seven **billion** people* | Billion stays singular |
| Without a number, meaning "many groups of 1,000,000,000" | ✅ **Countable** (with -s) | ***billions of** cells* | Add -s and use "of" |
| As a noun meaning "1,000,000,000-unit groups" | ✅ **Countable** | *The government spends **billions*** | Can be plural |

**Examples:**
- *The universe is **14 billion** years old.* (specific → no -s)
- ***Billions of** bacteria live in our gut.* (vague → add -s)
- *The project cost **billions**.* (vague plural noun)

## Complete Comparison Table

| Number Word | With Specific Number (No -s) | Vague / General (With -s + "of") | As a Plural Noun |
|-------------|------------------------------|----------------------------------|------------------|
| **hundred** | *two **hundred*** | ***hundreds of*** | *paid in **hundreds*** |
| **thousand** | *five **thousand*** | ***thousands of*** | *cost **thousands*** |
| **million** | *ten **million*** | ***millions of*** | *worth **millions*** |
| **billion** | *three **billion*** | ***billions of*** | *spends **billions*** |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| *five **thousands** dollars* | *five **thousand** dollars* | Specific number → no -s |
| ***thousand of** people* | ***thousands of** people* | No specific number → add -s + "of" |
| *two **millions** people* | *two **million** people* | Specific number → no -s |
| ***million of** stars* | ***millions of** stars* | No specific number → add -s + "of" |
| *She earned **billion of** dollars* | *She earned **billions of** dollars* | No specific number → add -s + "of" |

---

## Special Case: Using "a" Before Hundred/Thousand/Million/Billion

You can also use **"a"** to mean *"one"*:

| With "a" | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| ***a hundred*** | = *one hundred* (100) |
| ***a thousand*** | = *one thousand* (1,000) |
| ***a million*** | = *one million* (1,000,000) |
| ***a billion*** | = *one billion* (1,000,000,000) |

**Examples:**
- *A hundred people showed up.* (= one hundred)
- *He won **a million** dollars.* (= one million)

**Note:** When using "a," **never add -s** — it's still singular.

---

## When to Use "of" (Very Important!)

| Rule | Example |
|------|---------|
| **With a specific number** → NO "of" | *five **hundred** people* (NOT *five hundred of people*) |
| **With "hundreds/thousands/millions/billions" (plural)** → USE "of" | ***hundreds of** people* |
| **With "a"** → optional "of" | *a hundred people* OR *a hundred **of** them* (when followed by a pronoun or specific group) |

**Examples with "of" after "a":**
- *A hundred **of** them came.* (specific group)
- *I've read a hundred **of** these books.* (specific books)

But:
- *There are a hundred people here.* (general → no "of")

---

## Final Summary Checklist

| | With Specific Number | Vague / General |
|--|----------------------|-----------------|
| **Form** | *number + hundred/thousand/million/billion* | ***hundreds/thousands/millions/billions of*** |
| **Examples** | *three hundred / five thousand / ten million / two billion* | *hundreds of / thousands of / millions of / billions of* |
| **-s?** | ❌ No -s | ✅ Add -s |
| **"of"?** | ❌ No "of" | ✅ Add "of" after the plural |

---

## The One Rule to Remember Forever

> **If there's a number in front → NO -s, NO "of"** → *five hundred people*  
> **If there's NO number → ADD -s, ADD "of"** → *hundreds of people*

2. *______ (million) of people watched the game.*
3. *The car costs fifty ______ (thousand) dollars.*
4. *She has read ______ (hundred) of books.*
5. *The population is over eight ______ (billion).*

1. **thousand** (specific number → no -s)

4. **hundreds** (no number → add -s + "of")
5. **billion** (specific number → no -s)
6. **millions** (vague plural noun → add -s)
