# 1、Examples
- afraid of(害怕): Many kids **are** afraid of dogs that **are** not on a leash.
- ahead of(在……前面): We **have finished** our work ahead of the schedule that **was** set last month.
- aware of(知道): She **didn’t become** aware of the danger until someone **warned** her.
- capable of(能够): He **will be** capable of taking on more tasks once he **has trained** for a month.
- careful of/with(小心): Please **be** careful of the wet floor that they **just mopped**.
- certain of(确信): They **were** certain of the result before they **saw** the final report.
- conscious of(意识到): He **has been** conscious of his bad habits since he **started** to improve himself.
- envious of(妒忌): She **was** envious of her sister’s talent when she **was** young.
- fond of(爱好): My grandfather **has been** fond of collecting stamps since he **was** a child.
- guilty of(有……罪的): He **was found** guilty of the fraud that he **committed** two years ago.
- ignorant of(不了解): Many people **are** still ignorant of the facts that **have been** proven.
- independent of(独立于): The organization **has been** independent of the government since it **was founded**.
- jealous of(妒忌): Don’t **be** jealous of others just because they **have** something you don’t.
- kind of/to(对……和蔼): The nurse **was** very kind to the patients who **were** in pain.
- north/south/east/west of(在……的北/南/东/西面): The village **lies** west of the river that **runs** across the island.
- short of(缺乏): We **will be** short of money if we **don’t** raise more funds soon.
- shy of(顾虑): He **has always been** shy of speaking in public even though he **is** smart.
- sure of(肯定): I **was** not sure of the answer until I **checked** my notes.
- worthy of(值得): His courage **is** worthy of great respect that everyone **gives** him.---


# 2、Exercises
## 2.1 英文填空（时态+介词）
1. Many kids **were** afraid **of** the dark when they were little.
2. We **have been** already ahead **of** the plan that the manager made last week.
3. She **wasn’t** aware **of** the risk until she experienced it yesterday.
4. They **will be** capable **of** finishing the project ahead of time next month.
5. He **has been** always careful **of** his belongings since he lost his phone last year.
6. The team **was** certain **of** victory before the match started last Saturday.
7. My sister **was** conscious **of** her mistakes and decided to correct them.
8. I **have been** never envious **of** others’ success because I believe in my own effort.
9. My father **has been** fond **of** hiking since he was young.
10. The man **was** guilty **of** cheating and was punished last month.
11. She **was** ignorant **of** the truth until you told her just now.
12. This country **has been** independent **of** foreign control for over 50 years.
13. He **was** jealous **of** his brother’s talent when he was a teenager.
14. The teacher **is** always kind **to** every student who needs help.
15. The small town **lies** east **of** the lake that attracts many tourists.
16. We **will be** short **of** staff if more people don’t join us next week.
17. She **has been** shy **of** talking to strangers since she was a child.
18. I **am** sure **of** his honesty after I worked with him for years.
19. Their spirit **is** worthy **of** our praise forever.

## 2.2 中文翻译
1. 很多孩子小时候都怕黑。
2. 我们已经提前完成了经理上周制定的计划。

4. 他们下个月有能力提前完成这个项目。
5. 自从去年丢了手机，他一直很小心保管自己的物品。
6. 上周六比赛开始前，球队就确信会取得胜利。
7. 我姐姐意识到了自己的错误并决定改正。
8. 我从不妒忌别人的成功，因为我相信自己的努力。
9. 我爸爸从小就喜欢徒步旅行。
10. 这名男子犯了欺诈罪，上个月受到了处罚。
11. 直到你刚才告诉她，她才了解真相。
12. 这个国家已经独立于外国控制五十多年了。
13. 他十几岁时曾妒忌哥哥的天赋。
14. 这位老师对每一个需要帮助的学生都很和蔼。
15. 这座小镇在那个吸引众多游客的湖泊东面。
16. 如果下周没有更多人加入，我们就会缺人手。
17. 她从小就不好意思和陌生人说话。
18. 和他共事多年后，我肯定他为人诚实。
19. 他们的精神永远值得我们赞扬。---
